About Me

Hello and welcome to my website! I am currently enrolled at the University of Minnesota Coding Bootcamp and will be graduating in July 2019.


After working many years as an Application Systems Analyst I decided I wanted to expand on my knowledge and work more with coding. I received my computer science degree from St. Cloud State University so I have always had a deep passion for learning how to create and develop programs.


My goal is to utilize the skills learned in this class to further enhance the productivity of my coworkers and others around me. Making programs and technology enhance lives is something we can all aspire to!
